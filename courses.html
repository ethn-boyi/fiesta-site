Our Courses

Enhance your skills with our professional training programs.

Baking and Cake Decoration

Baking and cake decoration is both an art and a science that requires precision, creativity, and mastery of techniques. This course provides a comprehensive understanding of ingredients, mixing methods, and baking techniques essential for creating high-quality cakes, pastries, and bread. You will learn how to balance flavors, textures, and colors to create visually appealing and delicious baked goods. From simple sponge cakes to elaborate wedding cakes, our training covers everything from scratch baking to advanced decorating techniques using fondant, buttercream, and edible art. You will also gain insights into the business side of baking, including pricing, customer service, and marketing strategies. Whether you aspire to become a professional baker, run a home-based bakery, or improve your skills for personal enjoyment, this course provides the practical knowledge and hands-on experience needed to succeed in the competitive baking industry.

Pastry

Pastry-making is a delicate craft that combines skill, technique, and creativity to produce delicious and visually stunning desserts. This course will teach you how to make a variety of pastries, including puff pastry, choux pastry, shortcrust pastry, and laminated dough. You will learn essential skills such as dough preparation, layering, rolling, and baking to achieve the perfect texture and taste. Additionally, we cover advanced techniques in chocolate work, sugar art, and plated desserts, enabling you to elevate your pastry presentation. Whether you aim to work in a high-end pastry shop, start your own business, or simply impress with your baking skills, this course provides all the knowledge and hands-on experience you need.

Catering

Catering is more than just cooking; it’s about crafting an unforgettable culinary experience for different occasions. This course covers menu planning, food preparation, presentation, and event management. You will gain skills in preparing large-scale meals for weddings, corporate functions, and social gatherings while maintaining the highest hygiene and food safety standards. We also teach business aspects such as pricing, budgeting, and customer service to help you establish a thriving catering business. Whether you dream of running your own catering company or working in the hospitality industry, this course provides the perfect foundation.

Computer Packages

In today’s digital world, computer literacy is a must-have skill. Our computer packages course is designed to equip students with essential digital skills, from basic computer operations to advanced software applications. You will learn how to use Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint), internet navigation, email management, and basic graphic design. This course is ideal for students, professionals, and business owners who want to enhance their efficiency and productivity. Additionally, we introduce fundamental programming concepts and data management skills that are valuable in today’s job market.

Hair and beauty

The beauty industry is ever-evolving, and this course provides the skills needed to excel in hairdressing, skincare, and makeup artistry. You will learn haircutting techniques, styling, braiding, and coloring, as well as facial treatments, manicures, pedicures, and professional makeup application. Our course includes practical hands-on training to ensure you master the latest beauty trends and techniques. We also emphasize client consultation, customer service, and business management skills to help you establish a successful beauty career. Whether you want to work in a salon, start your own beauty business, or become a freelance stylist, this course provides the foundation you need.

Dress Making and fashion design

Fashion design is a dynamic field that blends creativity and technical skills. Our dressmaking and fashion design course covers everything from fabric selection and pattern-making to sewing and garment construction. You will learn how to create custom outfits, alter clothing, and design fashionable pieces for different occasions. The course also introduces students to fashion trends, color theory, and branding, equipping them with the skills to start their own fashion label or work in the industry. Whether you want to create elegant dresses, casual wear, or high-fashion designs, this course offers a solid foundation in the world of fashion.

Entrepreneurship and Life Skills

Success in any career requires more than just technical skills—it demands a strong entrepreneurial mindset and essential life skills. This course is designed to equip students with business knowledge, financial management skills, communication skills, and problem-solving abilities. We cover business planning, marketing strategies, and customer relations to help you start and grow a successful enterprise. Additionally, we focus on soft skills such as leadership, teamwork, and time management, which are critical for personal and professional development. Whether you plan to start your own business or improve your employability, this course provides invaluable skills for long-term success.
