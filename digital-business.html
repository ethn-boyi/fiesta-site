Business Digital Skills Course

Complete certification in Business Digital Skills and Online Entrepreneurship

Target Group: Youth, entrepreneurs, and professionals seeking practical digital skills for business

Outcome: Certificate in Business Digital Skills and Online Entrepreneurship

Month 1: Digital Foundations & Computer Literacy

  • Introduction to ICT
  • Importance of digital skills in business
  • Overview of hardware and software
  • Basic Computer Operations
  • File management, typing, and navigation
  • Operating systems and desktop tools
  • Internet & Email Use
  • Browsing, search engines, and safe practices
  • Creating and managing professional email accounts

Month 2: Office Productivity Tools

  • Microsoft Word / Google Docs
  • Document formatting, templates, and reports
  • Microsoft Excel / Google Sheets
  • Data entry, formulas, budgeting, and analysis
  • Microsoft PowerPoint / Google Slides
  • Creating business presentations and proposals
  • Cloud Storage & Collaboration
  • Using Google Drive, OneDrive, and shared folders

Month 3: Digital Communication & Marketing

  • Social Media for Business
  • Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp Business
  • Content creation and audience engagement
  • Digital Branding
  • Logos, color schemes, and brand identity
  • Email Marketing Basics
  • Mailchimp or similar tools
  • Building mailing lists and newsletters

Month 4: E-Commerce & Online Business Tools

  • Introduction to E-Commerce
  • Selling on Jumia, Kilimall, or social platforms
  • Product photography and descriptions
  • Mobile Money & Digital Payments

  • Data privacy, copyright, and responsible use

  • Launching a Digital Business
  • Steps to start, market, and grow online
  • Portfolio Development
  • Showcase of digital work: documents, designs, plans

  • Practical and theory assessments
  • Graduation and presentation of business ideas

Inclusive Beadwork Skills Course

Certificate in Inclusive Beadwork and Creative Enterprise

Target Group: Youth, women, and learners with diverse abilities seeking vocational and creative empowerment

Outcome: Certificate in Inclusive Beadwork and Creative Enterprise

Month 1: Introduction to Beadwork & Inclusion

  • Orientation & Values-Based Learning
  • Respect, patience, and collaboration in creative spaces
  • Understanding diverse learner needs and strengths
  • History & Cultural Significance of Beadwork
  • Kenyan beadwork traditions and symbolism
  • Global styles and applications
  • Basic Tools & Materials
  • Types of beads, threads, needles, and safety practices
  • Organizing inclusive workspaces

Month 2: Foundational Beading Techniques

  • Threading & Stitching Basics
  • Single-thread, double-thread, and loop techniques
  • Bead alignment and tension control
  • Simple Projects
  • Bracelets, keychains, and bookmarks
  • Color matching and pattern repetition
  • Supportive Group Work
  • Peer mentoring and collaborative creation
  • Encouraging learners with varied abilities

Month 3: Design & Pattern Development

  • Creative Thinking & Planning
  • Sketching beadwork designs
  • Using grids and templates
  • Color Theory & Symbolism
  • Cultural meanings and emotional impact
  • Inclusive design choices
  • Intermediate Projects
  • Necklaces, earrings, and decorative bands

Month 4: Advanced Techniques & Customization

  • Layering & 3D Beadwork
  • Multi-strand designs and raised patterns
  • Personalized Items
  • Name tags, initials, and custom motifs
  • Inclusive Teaching Practices
  • Adapting tools and methods for learners with disabilities
  • Building confidence through guided repetition

Month 5: Product Development & Community Engagement

  • Creating Market-Ready Items
  • Packaging, finishing, and presentation
  • Group Projects & Exhibitions
  • Collaborative murals, wall hangings, or themed collections
  • Community Outreach
  • Beadwork for awareness campaigns or cultural events
  • Inclusive storytelling through design

Month 6: Entrepreneurship & Final Assessment

  • Business Skills for Creatives
  • Pricing, budgeting, and customer service
  • Selling via social media and local markets
  • Portfolio & Showcase Preparation
  • Documenting work and presenting to stakeholders
  • Final Practical & Theory Exams
  • Inclusive assessment methods
  • Graduation and certificate issuance
