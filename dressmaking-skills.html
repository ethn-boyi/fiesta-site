Professional Dressmaking Course

Our comprehensive 6-month Dressmaking and Fashion Craft course is designed to transform your passion for fashion into professional expertise. This hands-on program combines traditional tailoring techniques with modern design principles, ensuring you graduate as a skilled dressmaker ready to launch your career in the fashion industry.

Whether you aspire to start your own tailoring business, work in fashion houses, or simply master the art of creating beautiful garments, this course provides the perfect foundation for success in the dynamic world of fashion and garment construction.

Introduction to Dressmaking
