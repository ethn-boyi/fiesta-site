Our comprehensive 6-month Beauty Therapy course is designed to equip you with the essential skills and knowledge needed to excel in the beauty and wellness industry. 6-Month Curriculum Breakdown

A comprehensive journey from basics to professional mastery

1

Introduction & Personal Grooming

Foundation skills and professional standards

Orientation & Professional Conduct

  • Salon hygiene, ethics, and client care
  • Personal presentation and grooming standards

Skin Anatomy & Physiology

  • Layers of the skin and their functions
  • Common skin types and conditions

Basic Facial Treatments

  • Cleansing, toning, exfoliation, and moisturizing
  • Product selection and application techniques
2

Skin Care & Facial Therapy

Advanced facial treatments and massage techniques

Advanced Facial Techniques

  • Steam therapy, extraction, and mask application
  • Anti-aging and acne treatments

Massage Techniques

  • Facial massage movements and benefits
  • Relaxation and lymphatic drainage

Client Consultation & Record Keeping

  • Skin analysis and treatment planning
  • Documentation and follow-up care
3

Nail Care & Hand/Foot Therapy

Comprehensive manicure, pedicure, and nail artistry

Manicure & Pedicure Basics

  • Nail shaping, cuticle care, buffing, and polishing
  • Hygiene and safety procedures

Nail Art & Enhancements

  • Decorative techniques and trends
  • Use of gels, acrylics, and tips

Hand & Foot Massage

  • Pressure points and relaxation techniques

Hair Removal & Eye Treatments

Professional hair removal and eye enhancement services

Waxing Techniques

  • Hot and cold wax application
  • Facial and body hair removal

Threading & Tweezing

  • Eyebrow shaping and upper lip threading

Eye Treatments

  • Eyelash tinting, eyebrow grooming
  • Application of false lashes

Makeup Application & Bridal Styling

Professional makeup artistry for all occasions

Day, Evening & Bridal Makeup

  • Foundation matching, contouring, and highlighting
  • Eye shadow blending and lip techniques

Makeup for Different Skin Tones

  • Cultural sensitivity and client preferences

Special Occasion Styling

  • Bridal consultations and trial sessions
  • Hair and makeup coordination

Salon Management & Entrepreneurship

Business skills and professional certification

Salon Setup & Operations

Customer Service & Marketing

Business Planning & Financial Literacy

Final Assessment & Certification

  • Practical exam and theory test
  • Portfolio presentation and graduation
