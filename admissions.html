Admission Without Formal Qualifications

Inclusive training opportunities for every learner

Home / About / Admission Requirements

Learning Beyond Barriers

At Fiesta Trainers and Caterers, we welcome learners who may not have KCSE or formal schooling. Our inclusive programs are designed to empower individuals with practical, income-generating skills based on interest, aptitude, and readiness.

Accessible Courses

Dressmaking & Tailoring

Open to all, even without KCSE. Build your future in garment construction and fashion design.

  • Basic literacy preferred
  • Interest in fashion
  • Careers: tailoring, uniform production, fashion entrepreneurship

Home Care Training

No formal education required. Ideal for compassionate learners ready to support families.

  • Good interpersonal skills
  • Age 16+ recommended
  • Careers: nanny, housekeeper, caregiver, hospitality support

Beadwork & Jewelry Design

Fully open entry. Express creativity and craftsmanship for self-employment or artisan markets.

  • Hand-eye coordination
  • No academic requirements
  • Careers: jewelry design, artisan crafts

Special Needs Training

Open to caregivers & community workers with empathy and patience.

  • Basic communication skills
  • No prior experience required
  • Careers: NGO work, inclusive classroom support, caregiving

Catering

Passion for food? No academic requirements — just commitment to hygiene and cooking.

  • Basic hygiene knowledge
  • Ability to follow instructions
  • Careers: cook, catering assistant, food vendor

Baking Technology

Learn professional baking and pastry skills. Open to all passionate learners.

  • Creativity & interest required
  • Basic literacy helpful
  • Careers: bakery assistant, pastry chef, bakery entrepreneur

Cake Decoration

Turn your artistic flair into a career in event and wedding cake design.

  • Hand-eye coordination
  • No academic requirements
  • Careers: event cake designer, freelance pastry artist

General Admission Process

Fill out application form
Provide ID or birth certificate
Attend orientation/interview
Submit passport-size photos
